Overview of Comprehensive Platinum Full Body Checkup

How Comprehensive Platinum Full Body Checkup helps you?

Unhealthy lifestyle and stress can gradually take a toll on our health. Early detection can help to capture the warning signs of masked diseases in the body. Comprehensive Platinum Full Body Checkup Package provides a comprehensive range of tests that check your liver, heart and kidney function, blood sugar, thyroid status, digestive health, immune system, lipid profile, blood profile, vitamins, minerals, urine and more.In addition to all the features of Comprehensive Gold Full Body Checkup Package, it also provides cardiac risk markers, pancreatic enzymes and allergy tests.This package – a part of our ‘premium range’ of diagnostic tests – can be ordered once every 6 to 12 months or as recommended by your doctor.

Precautions

Do not eat or drink anything other than water for 8-12 hours before the test.
